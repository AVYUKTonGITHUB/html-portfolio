The BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO AVYUKT

Avyukt's All Time Top Movies

1. INCEPTION, 2010

In the movie, Nolan wanted to explore "the idea of people sharing a dream space... That gives you the ability to access somebody's unconscious mind. What would that be used and abused for?" The majority of the film's plot takes place in these interconnected dream worlds. This structure creates a framework where actions in the real or dream worlds ripple across others. Directed by Christopher Nolan.
*Casting Dicaprio, Joseph Gordon Levitt, Elliot Page, Cilian Murphy, Tom Hardy and many more.
*Also got Awarded in 83rd ACADEMY AWARD as Best in Cinematography, Sound Editing, Sound Mixing and Visula Effects.

2.ALL QUIET ON WESTERN FRONT, 2022

All Quiet on the Western Front (German: Im Westen nichts Neues, lit. 'In the West nothing new') is a 2022 German epic anti-war film based on the 1929 novel of the same name by Erich Maria Remarque.It is the third film adaptation of the book, after the 1930 and 1979 versions. Directed by Edward Berger.
*Casting Felix Kammerer, Albrecht Schuch, Daniel Brühl, Sebastian Hülk, Aaron Hilmer, Edin Hasanovic and Devid Striesow.
*ALso got Awarded in 95th ACADEMY AWARD in Cinematography, Internation Feature Film, Production Design and Original Score.

3.CHHICHHORE, 2019

Chhichhore (transl. Frivolous) is a 2019 Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age comedy-drama film. Based on Tiwari's experiences as a student of the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, also the Director of the movie.
*Casting Sushant Singh Rajput, Shraddha Kapoor, Varun Sharma, Tahir Raj Bhasin, Naveen Polishetty, Tushar Pandey and Saharsh Kumar Shukla in the lead roles.
*Also got Awarded in 67th NATIONAL FILM AWARDS in Best Feature Film(Hindi)

4.TOP GUN: MAVERICK, 2022

Set 30 years after its predecessor, it follows Maverick's return to the United States Navy Strike Fighter Tactics Instructor program (also known as U.S. Navy-Fighter Weapons School - "TOPGUN"), where he must confront his past as he trains a group of younger pilots, among them the son of Maverick's deceased best friend Lieutenant Nick "Goose" Bradshaw, USN. Directed by Joseph Kosinski.
*Casting Tom Cruise, Jinnifer Connelly, Miles Teller and many more.
*Also got Awarded 95th ACADEMY AWARD in Best Sound.

5.THREE IDIOTS, 2009

The film is based on novel Five Point Someone by Chetan BhagatRancho is an engineering student. His two friends. Farhan and Raju, Rancho sees the world in a different way. Rancho goes somewhere one day. And his friends find him. When Rancho is found, he has become one of a great scientist in the world.Directed by RajKumar Hirnai.
*Casting Aamir Khan, R.Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, Omi Vaidya, Boman Irani , Mona Singh and many more.
*Also got Awarded 55th FILMFARE AWARDS in Best(Film, Director,Dialogue,Story,Screenplay,SupportingActor) and 57th NATIONAL FILM AWARDS in Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment and in Best Lyrics. The Film won 47 Awards in 67 Nominations in All Catogories.
